Information System and Database
After learned the normalization of a database, we learn the different relationship between tables. There are three different possible relationship between the tables: one to one, one to many and many to many. One to one happens when one element of a table is correlated to only one element of another table. One to many happens when one element of a table is correlated with many other elements of another table. Many to many happen when many elements of a table are correlated with many other elements of another table.
